Recording

Whether you use Garage Band, Logic, Ableton, Pro Tools, Reason, or just a microphone and recording device, recording music is difficult and takes some organization and practice. This page is intended to provide some tips and tricks for using various music software programs and also just general formulas and strategies for being productive when composing.

This might include:

-Settings for recording instruments, good effects per instrument, EQ and mastering techniques and recommendations.

-Sample packs and files of interest.

-Start with melody, then drum, then bass, etc....

-There is still a lot of work to do here. I'd like to make a "getting started" cheat sheet for each DAW and software.
