Introduction to the Site

Welcome keyboardists, pianists, and guitar players! This site is intended as a means of support for anyone interested in writing songs. In an attempt to make this website a one-stop shop, I've broken down the content into major categories of interest. The home page will be a place to check for updated blog posts, which will include links to relevant articles, new songs, videos, and more. Take a look around at the rest of the site to find hopefully some interesting content that could be educational and helpful as you embark on creating your next composition of music. Enjoy!
